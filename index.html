ABOUT US

Origin

Grassrootsclub was founded in 2013, is the environmental club at John Cabot University.

Name

The name itself reflects both our connection to the Earth, as well as our ground-up approach to tackling environmental issues.

Motto

As agents of progress and as a social force in the university pretends a better worls, our motto is "Be the Change!"

Coworking

Our Mission

Global Sustainability

The mission of Grassroots, as provided by its constitution, is to:

Educate students and faculty members on environmental issues.

Evaluate and make suggestions to the University on how to make its facilities greener and more sustainable.

Work and interact with environmental NGOs.

Participate in environmental volunteer programs already established in Rome and Lazio.

Bring the student body and faculty members closer to nature while teaching how to better respect our surroundings.

Promote general self-awareness and well-being.
